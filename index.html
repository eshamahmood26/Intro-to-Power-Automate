What is Power Automate?

Power Automate is a workflow automation platform that enables us to streamline processes, reduce manual effort, and improve operational efficiency by seamlessly integrating with our existing Microsoft ecosystem.

Connectors

Sharepoint, OneDrive, Outlook, Teams, Adobe and many more.

Triggers

Events that trigger the workflow (e.g., "When an email arrives" or "Daily at 8:00 AM").

Actions

The Execution steps (e.g., "Request Approval", "Save a copy of the email attachment here", "Extract infomation from the email and fill out the excel sheet", "Send an email/teams notification").

3 Understanding Triggers In-Depth

What is a Trigger?

A Trigger is the starting point of your flow. It's the event that automatically kickstarts your entire automation process without any manual intervention.

Trigger Flow:

1. Something happens (email arrives, file created, date/time occurs)
2. Power Automate detects it immediately
3. The workflow activates automatically
4. Actions execute in sequence

Common Trigger Examples

When an email arrives

From Outlook or Gmail

On a scheduled time

Daily, weekly, or monthly at specific times

When a file is created

In SharePoint or OneDrive

When an item is modified

In SharePoint lists or in an Excel table

When a response is submitted

From Forms or approval requests

AI vs. Automation

While often used interchangeably, Artificial Intelligence and Process Automation serve distinct operational purposes.

Process Automation

  • Rule-Based Execution: Operates on strict, predefined rules "If X, then Y".
  • High Predictability: Follows exact instructions consistently without deviation or interpretation.
  • Best Applied For: Repetitive data entry, routing approvals, and notifications and reminders.

Artificial Intelligence

  • Data-Driven Learning: Analyzes patterns, unstructured data, and context to generate outcomes.
  • Adaptive Logic: AI enhances processes by learning from data, generating insights, and supporting decision-making
  • Best Applied For: Sentiment analysis, document processing, and AI Agents.

AI Features in Power Automate: Power Automate acts can move and extract data, while allowing integration with tools like AI Builder and Copilot Agents.
